Ghost Types

Learn about different ghost types and their characteristics

Spirit

  • EMF Level 5
  • Spirit Box
  • Ghost Writing

A common ghost that can be encountered anywhere. They are powerful but passive, and smudge sticks are effective against them.

Wraith

  • EMF Level 5
  • Spirit Box
  • D.O.T.S Projector

One of the most dangerous ghosts. They can fly and travel through walls. Salt is ineffective against Wraiths.

Phantom

  • Spirit Box
  • Fingerprints
  • D.O.T.S Projector

A ghost that can possess the living. Taking photos of the Phantom will make it temporarily disappear.

Poltergeist

  • Spirit Box
  • Fingerprints
  • Ghost Writing

A ghost that can manipulate objects around it. They are known to throw multiple objects at once.

Banshee

  • Ghost Orb
  • Fingerprints
  • D.O.T.S Projector

A natural hunter that targets one person at a time. They fear the Crucifix and will be less aggressive near one.

Jinn

  • EMF Level 5
  • Fingerprints
  • Freezing Temperatures

A territorial ghost that will use electrical power to move at incredible speeds. Turning off the location's power source will prevent this ability.

Mare

  • Spirit Box
  • Ghost Orb
  • Ghost Writing

The source of all nightmares, they are stronger in the dark. Turning the lights on will reduce their aggressive behavior.

Revenant

  • Ghost Orb
  • Ghost Writing
  • Freezing Temperatures

A slow but violent ghost that will travel at significantly faster speeds when hunting their prey. Hiding from the Revenant will cause it to move very slowly.

Shade

  • EMF Level 5
  • Ghost Writing
  • Freezing Temperatures

A shy ghost that will rarely perform actions in the presence of multiple people. Being shy makes it harder to find but also makes it less aggressive.

Demon

  • Fingerprints
  • Ghost Writing
  • Freezing Temperatures

One of the worst ghosts to encounter. They are known to attack without reason and using a Crucifix is essential for defense.

Yurei

  • Ghost Orb
  • Freezing Temperatures
  • D.O.T.S Projector

A ghost that has returned to the physical world, usually for the purpose of revenge or hatred. Smudging the room will trap it temporarily.

Oni

  • EMF Level 5
  • Freezing Temperatures
  • D.O.T.S Projector

Extremely active and aggressive ghosts that are more likely to appear in physical form. Their activity increases with more people nearby.
